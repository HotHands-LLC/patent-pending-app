## Terms

### 1. Appointment as Non-Exclusive Agent

Patent Owner(s) appoint HHLLC as a **non-exclusive** licensing agent for the above-identified patent. HHLLC may list the patent on its platform and facilitate introductions to potential licensees. This appointment does not prevent Patent Owner(s) from pursuing licensing independently or through other channels.

### 2. Ownership Retained

Patent Owner(s) retain all rights, title, and interest in the patent. Nothing in this Agreement transfers ownership, assigns any patent rights, or grants HHLLC any license to practice the invention. HHLLC's sole role is to facilitate introductions and assist with deal execution.

### 3. Commission

HHLLC earns a commission of **10% of the gross licensing fee** on any deal **closed through the PatentPending platform**. "Closed through the platform" means a deal initiated by an introduction or inquiry that originated through patentpending.app's Deal Page feature.

- No commission is owed on deals Patent Owner(s) close independently, through prior contacts, or through other channels.
- No upfront fees, listing fees, or retainer fees are charged.
- Commission is payable within 30 days of Patent Owner(s) receiving payment from the licensee.

### 4. No Exclusivity

This Agreement is non-exclusive. Patent Owner(s) are free to license their patent through any other agent, broker, marketplace, or directly at any time, without any obligation to HHLLC for those transactions.

### 5. Termination / Opt-Out

Either party may terminate this Agreement with **30 days written notice** to the other. Written notice may be delivered by email. Termination does not affect commission owed on deals already closed prior to the termination date.

- HHLLC termination notice to: the email on record for Patent Owner(s)
- Patent Owner termination notice to: support@hotdeck.com

### 6. Representations

Patent Owner(s) represent that they have the authority to enter into this Agreement and that, to the best of their knowledge, the patent is valid and they hold the rights necessary to license it.

HHLLC makes no guarantee of licensing success, minimum deal value, or timeline. The platform is provided as-is.

### 7. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the **State of Texas**, without regard to conflict of law principles. Any disputes shall be resolved in Lubbock County, Texas.

### 8. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding its subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions. Amendments require written consent of all parties.
